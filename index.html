    ZambaLes Swimming Team started at year 2011. Founded of Coach William "Biboy" Asturias.

    Our Mission

    We are the Zambales Swimming Team, We provided excellence and dedicated to developing the finest of athletes as well as the finest of citizens through the sport of swimming and our swimming program.

    Our Plan

    Our Vision

    Services



    Learn How To Swim Program for Beginners

    We are committed to providing the best learning experience for swimmers of all ages and abilities.

    Fitness & Trainings for Elite Swimmmer

    We offer a Different Spots programs that allow swimmers to participate in classes and even the interschool program. Novice swimmers may join our learn-to-swim and squad programs.

    Swim lesson for kid's and Toddler's

    Swim lessons can help reduce drowning risk for children between ages 1-4. Classes that include both parents and their children also are a good way to introduce good water safety habits and start building swim readiness skills.
